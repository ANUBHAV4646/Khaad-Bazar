🌿 Neem Cake Fertilizer

🏠 Home
Organic Insect Repellent Soil Conditioner

🧪 Requirements

  • Neem seeds or kernel
  • Oil extraction machinery
  • Drying and crushing tools
  • Compost mixer (optional)

🔧 How to Make Neem Cake Fertilizer

Step Action
1Collect neem seeds and dry them thoroughly.
2Extract oil using mechanical pressing.
3The residue (neem cake) is collected and dried.
4Grind into powder or apply directly to soil.
5Can be mixed with compost for added benefits.

⏱ Time Required

Preparation and processing takes 7 to 10 days.

🌟 Advantages

  • Acts as a natural pest and nematode repellent
  • Improves soil fertility and microbial activity
  • Suppresses harmful fungi and bacteria
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable

💰 Estimated Price

Neem cake fertilizer generally costs around ₹25 to ₹40 per kg.
