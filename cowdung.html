🐄Cow Dung Compost

🏠 Home
Organic Traditional Soil Enhancer

🧪 Requirements

  • Fresh cow dung
  • Dry agricultural waste or straw
  • Composting pit or drum
  • Water and shade
  • Aeration tools (optional)

🔧 How to Make Cow Dung Compost

Step Action
1Mix cow dung with dry waste in equal ratio.
2Layer the mixture in a pit or compost bin.
3Moisten the mixture slightly and keep it covered.
4Turn the pile every 10–15 days for aeration.
5After 45–60 days, compost will turn dark and granular.

⏱ Time Required

Approximately 45 to 60 days for complete decomposition.

🌟 Advantages

  • Improves soil fertility and structure
  • Increases microbial activity in soil
  • Reduces chemical fertilizer dependency
  • Utilizes animal waste productively

💰 Estimated Price

The average market price of cow dung compost is around ₹5 to ₹10 per kg, depending on processing and packaging.
