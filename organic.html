🌱 Organic Fertilizer

🏠 Home
Natural Nutrient-rich Environment Friendly

🧪 Requirements

  • Organic materials (manure, compost, green waste)
  • Fermentation or composting area
  • Water and sunlight
  • Proper mixing tools

🔧 How to Make Organic Fertilizer

Step Action
1Collect green waste and animal manure.
2Mix in a compost bin or open pit with regular moisture.
3Allow fermentation and decomposition for 30-60 days.
4Stir every 10-15 days to ensure aeration and breakdown.
5When dark and soil-like, it's ready for use.

⏱ Time Required

Usually 30 to 60 days depending on materials and climate.

🌟 Advantages

  • Improves soil health and structure
  • Reduces need for chemical fertilizers
  • Supports plant growth naturally
  • Cost-effective and sustainable
