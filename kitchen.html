🍽️ Kitchen Waste Compost

🏠 Home
Eco-Friendly Waste Utilization Home-based

🧪 Requirements

  • Vegetable and fruit peels
  • Used tea leaves, eggshells
  • Compost bin with lid
  • Dry leaves or newspaper
  • Water and stirring stick

🔧 How to Make Kitchen Waste Compost

Step Action
1Collect daily kitchen waste in a bin.
2Add dry leaves or newspaper as carbon material.
3Mix and keep the bin covered with small holes for air.
4Stir every few days to aid decomposition.
5Ready compost in 30-40 days with earthy smell.

⏱ Time Required

Usually 30 to 40 days depending on material and climate.

🌟 Advantages

  • Reduces household waste
  • Improves garden soil naturally
  • Prevents pollution from organic waste
  • Low-cost and easy to maintain

💰 Estimated Price

Homemade compost is usually free, but commercial versions cost ₹4 to ₹8 per kg.
