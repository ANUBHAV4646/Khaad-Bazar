🌿 Vermicompost

🏠 Home
Organic Eco-friendly Nutrient-rich

🧪 Requirements

  • Earthworms (Eisenia fetida)
  • Organic waste (vegetable peels, leaves, paper)
  • Moisture-retaining bin or pit
  • Partial shade or shelter
  • Proper aeration

🔧 How to Make Vermicompost

Step Action
1Prepare a bin or pit with partial shade and ventilation.
2Place a bedding layer (coconut coir, paper, dry leaves).
3Add organic waste in layers (avoid oily/spicy food).
4Introduce earthworms and lightly moisten the mixture.
5Cover with jute sack and maintain moisture regularly.
6After 40-50 days, dark, granular compost will be ready.

⏱ Time Required

Approximately 40 to 50 days under optimal conditions.

🌟 Advantages

  • Improves soil texture and water retention
  • Boosts microbial activity in the soil
  • Completely organic and chemical-free
  • Reduces household waste
